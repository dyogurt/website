A collection of icons inspired by the logo of CONGENTO°, a project that offers customized services in animal research. These icons represent animal models and services and have been used on the main website, printed materials°, and general communication.

The logo for QuantOCancer°, a quantitative biomedicine research program focused on cancer. Individual circles are used to represent quantity and the ones in the center are shown dividing, like cancer.

The logo for the projectAr, a brand new spin-off of the outreach initiative Ar | Respire connosco°. A thought-provoking film is screened, followed by an open conversation that will hopefully lead to positive social action. The logo represents, most directly, a projection, converting smaller into bigger. But it also expresses an expansion of ideas, words, and responses.
